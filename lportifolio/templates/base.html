Hi there and welcome to my website!

Here you'll find some information about who I am as a person, what drives and motivates me, as well as the skillset I've been developing throughout the years.

Shortly, I'm Larissa and I was born in São Paulo, Brazil. At the age of 17y, I was given the thrilling opportunity to come to Germany and study. I din't know how to speak German or know anyone there really... but as challenges make me sparkle, I decided to give it a shot. Fast forward a couple of years and here I am, still in Germany, based in Hamburg and driving digitization and the implementation of emerging technologies.

Of course, this wouldn't have been possible without the support of many people along the way that believed in my talent and helped me thrive, for whom I am more than thankful. With this platform, I aim to reach two main goals:

  1. Connect with and support those interested in following a similar path;
  2. Connect with stakeholders interested in or already implementing one or more technologies I use myself.

I can't wait to exchange ideas and building the future together with you!
